What is a Marketing Director and What Do They Do?

A Marketing Director is in charge of managing all aspects related to the production and implementation of a campaign. They're responsible for strategizing and analyzing and leading their team while making sure everything runs smoothly from start to finish with clients.



What are some skills someone in this position should have?

  • Communication
  • Strategy
  • Research
  • Creativity
  • Interpersonal Skills
  • Campaign
  • Leadership
  • Technical Skills
  • Project Management

What levels of education are needed for this job?

To become a marketing director, you will need a bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, English or a related field. Some marketing directors also have MBAs or other additional forms of certification.

What is the average salary of a Marketing Director?

The average salary of a marketing director in the US is $160k-$240k per year. Marketing director salaries have a wide, which depends on many significant factors, like education, certifications, additional skills, and the number of years you have spent in your profession.
