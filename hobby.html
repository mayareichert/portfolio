My Hobby


What is my hobby?

My favorite hobby to do is baking because it's fun and I can be creative with recipes. The process of mixing ingredients is satisfying and whatever I make usually smells really good. I also love sharing what I've made with my friends and family.It's a great way to have fun when you're bored, or to satisfy your hunger.

When did Baking start to be my hobby?

My hobby of baking all started when I was a toddler. I would always see my mom baking something for me and my sister, and I wanted to join in and help her. Ever since then, my mom and I still occasionally bake things together.

Some of my favorite recipes to make:

  • Chocolate Chip Cookies
  • Red Velvet Cupcakes
  • Snickerdoodles
  • Brownies
  • Blondies
  • Tiramasu
  • Cheesecake
  • Sugar Cookies
